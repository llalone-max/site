01 · Brands (B2C)

Two brands.

Healthcare is my career. Consumer brands are what I build alongside it.

Each one started the same way. I noticed something I wished were different, then built the thing that changes it, and shipped it. One is a sports app, live on the App Store. One is a cultural commentary show, with the clips cut and published by pipelines that run mostly on their own.

It is a different skill set from healthcare, but they overlap more than you would expect. Building my own brands has been by far the fastest way to educate myself on new technology, and I am always learning, and always revising how much I think one person can do with a good idea.
